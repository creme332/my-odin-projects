Home-made KFC meal

Description

A tasty concoction of chicken fillet, chutney, and chips supercharged with ketchup and mayonnaise.

Rumor has it that this was the last meal of Jesus before his ressurection.

Cooking time 10 mins.

Caloric intake per serving : 10

Ingredients

Serves a single lucky person

  • 1 chicken head with beak (preferably from a dead chicken)
  • Freshly ordered chips from McDonalds
  • 2 quarts neutral oil, for frying
  • 1 bottle of ketchup
  • 1 bottle of mayonnaise
  • Leftover chutney from 5 years ago before grandma died.

  4. Fry chicken. Chicken is done when a meat thermometer inserted into the beak part reads "what the fuck?".
  5. Let chicken drain on a few paper towels when it comes out of the fryer. Serve hot.
